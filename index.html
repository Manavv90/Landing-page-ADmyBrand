What is ADmyBRAND AI Suite?

ADmyBRAND AI Suite is a comprehensive marketing automation platform powered by artificial intelligence. It helps businesses create, optimize, and manage their marketing campaigns across multiple channels with intelligent automation and data-driven insights.

How does the AI automation work?

Our AI analyzes your audience behavior, campaign performance, and market trends to automatically optimize your campaigns. It adjusts targeting, timing, content, and budget allocation in real-time to maximize your ROI.

What integrations are available?

We integrate with over 200+ popular tools including CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot), email platforms (Mailchimp, Constant Contact), social media platforms, Google Analytics, and major e-commerce platforms.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade or cancel anytime during or after the trial period.

What support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including email support for all plans, priority support for Pro users, and dedicated account managers for Enterprise clients. We also offer extensive documentation, video tutorials, and live training sessions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
